## 4. Notes for Processing

- **PR-03** (Sarah Byrne's internal note) contains personal data of *both* Adam (subject of the comment) and, arguably, opinion data attributable to Sarah as author — flag for third-party/mixed-data review.
- **EM-01, EM-04, EM-05** all involve internal discussions *about* Adam between Tom and Sarah — these are Adam's personal data (they relate to and affect him), but may also require redaction of purely internal deliberation not required for disclosure, subject to legal review.
- **EM-04 and EM-05** together are the clearest documentary link between the automated score and the redundancy decision — this is the core Article 22 evidence trail.
- **PR-04** (system config log) is not personal data about Adam specifically, but is essential context for explaining "the logic involved" under Article 15(1)(h) — likely belongs in the explanation Nordwave gives Adam, not necessarily as a raw disclosed record.
